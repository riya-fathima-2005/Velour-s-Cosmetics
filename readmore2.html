Choosing the Right Eyebrow Palette

Key Factors from Experts:

An eyeliner pencil is a cosmetic tool used to define and accentuate
the eyes. It typically comes in a wood or plastic-encased stick and is considered a
beginner-friendly option due to its ease of control.

Other Helpful tips:

Match shades to your eye color:

  • Eyeliner pencils are available in different formulas, each with unique characteristics.
  • Traditional kohl/wax pencils: These have a waxy texture that can be warmed up to apply smoothly. They
  • are excellent for creating soft, smokey, and smudged looks. Gel eyeliner pencils: These are formulated to have the smooth,
  • pot-and-brush gel liner but with the ease of a pencil. They are often long-lasting and waterproof.Retractable pencils: These twist up,
  • eliminating the need for a sharpener. They are convenient for quick and easy application.

Skin Tone Matching:

Fair skin: Rose, champagne, plum.
Medium: Burgundy, pink shimmer, lavender, or gray.
Olive: Gold, bronze, navy, emerald.
Deeper skin: Bold blues, purples, burgundy, or glitter.
